Ted Lasso

About Ted

Ted Lasso is a charismatic American college football coach who was hired by the owner of an English soccer club with the intent to crush her ex husband's dream. He thwarted her plan by turning over impossible wins for the Richmond team. He wasn't welcome at first. He was mocked and teased in private and public for his incompetency in English football. Yet he kept pushing. With enough motivational poster styled dialogue, Ted successfully won over Rebecca's friendship and the admiration of once hateful and unruly young men who now became successful professional players.

Characteristics

Friends

Ted is a character driven almost entirely by his desire to support the people he loves. Whether it's a dating mishap or a multi-million dollar game, Ted is always there to give a pep talk or a push. Besides the young men he coaches, Ted has a team of people who are there to support him. Not all of them started as friends but none of these people could resist Ted's charm.
