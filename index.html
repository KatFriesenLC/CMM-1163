WHO ARE THEY?

Kat is a first-year multimedia production student at the Lethbridge College. They have worked on marketing projects for local organizations and are currently involved in the operations of a local pet store while in school. They found their passion for the world of design after helping small businesses with their online presence and showing them the valuable results that can come with social media marketing. During their time at the college they have already gained enough skills to know the world of design is the place they want to stay.

THIS IS ROW 2

Growing up in rural Alberta, Kat spent more of their time with animals than with people. They had the privilege of raising rabbits, goats, cows, horses, dogs, cats, and whatever neglected animal their mother found at the auction. Spending hours brushing, training, feeding and rehabilitating so many critters planted a deep passion for the well-being of all living beings. One day, they hope to get involved with organizations for wildlife education and rehabilitation to contribute to the conservation of local ecosystems.
A combination of international travel, language learning, and pleasure reading are the essential tools in Kat’s marketable communication kit. They grew up in a bilingual family, temporarily moved to El Salvador as soon as they turned 18 and spent a few years studying English at The King’s University. Learning more about the ways different people choose to communicate verbally and visually along the way. Free writing, storytelling and poetry are a regular practice that continues to build their ability to write interesting and effective material.
Their favourite way to refresh their mind is exploring the outdoors with their dog, Rosa. Everything from mountain tops to aisles of a hardware store has the potential to inspire. The crunch of autumn leaves, the sight of an overcast city, and the sound of squealing tires are some of their favourite sounds. Capturing the dynamics of life with images, video, and poetry are a few of their favourite things.
